About us

∎

Filipinos are braced and known for their optimism and resilience, but that does not alter the reality that these Filipinos are in dire need of help through financial assistance, educational governance, and health-related support. With that, iVolunteerPH connects nonprofit charitable organizations to a singular or group of users that aims to become their volunteer, donor, or official member. At the same time, our project also supports starting nonprofits to create their project, form a team, and start a fundraiser through us.
